## 📝 Name Meaning & Branding

### **Farmsetu** = **Farm** + **Setu** (Bridge)
- **Farm**: Represents agriculture, farmers, and rural economy
- **Setu**: Sanskrit/Hindi word meaning "Bridge" or "Connection"
- **Tagline**: "Connecting Farms Directly to Markets"

### Brand Identity:
- 🌾 **Agriculture Focus**: Emphasizes farming roots
- 🌉 **Bridge Concept**: Connects producers to markets
- 🇮🇳 **Indian Heritage**: Uses familiar Hindi terminology
- 💚 **Green Theme**: Growth, prosperity, nature
